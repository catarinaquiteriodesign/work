About me

Crafting beauty
with purpose.

I'm Catarina — a 27-year-old Product Designer and CGI Artist with a lifelong passion for creativity. From my early days of meticulously hand-drawn photorealistic portraits, I've been captivated by the power of visual artistry.

Inspired by the transformative world of CGI in film, I harness the potential of digital tools to bring ideas to life. My mission is to design products and spaces that resonate with people's emotions and memories.

What I do

What I
bring.

Six disciplines, one vision: beautiful things that feel alive.

01

Product & Furniture Design

Concept development from brief to final design. Full 3D modelling and specification-ready outputs for furniture and luxury product clients.

02

3D Modelling & CGI

Photorealistic visualisations using industry-leading tools. Products, interiors, and architectural exteriors rendered to near-photograph quality.

03

Environment & Lifestyle Imagery

Full scene construction staging products in rich context — boutiques, living rooms, outdoor spaces — for catalogue, press, and commercial use.

04

Architectural Visualisation

Exterior and interior renders for architects and developers. Experienced with large-scale residential complexes and high-end hospitality projects.

05

Ad Campaign Concepts

Product photography reimagined in CGI — creating campaign imagery that blends real-world references with digitally crafted atmospheres.

06

Brand Collaboration

Ongoing design partnerships with furniture and lifestyle brands, supporting product launches from concept sketch through to press-ready imagery.
